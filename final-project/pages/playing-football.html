We would support our local team and try to imitate their playing style. I remember one time, we even organized a football match between two neighborhoods. Nowadays, I still enjoy playing football. I often play with my friends in the park. We usually have a lot of fun. Playing football is a great way to stay fit and active. At the end football is my passion and will always be one of my favorite sports.
